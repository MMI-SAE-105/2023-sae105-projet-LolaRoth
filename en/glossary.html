Glossary

presentation of characters

Dom Cobb, played by Leonardo Di Caprio, is the central character of the film and a brilliant dream extractor.

He has unparalleled expertise in the art of penetrating the dreams of others to steal information.

However, it is haunted by a tumultuous past, including the loss of his wife Mal.

Mal Cobb is a complex and central character in the plot of Inception. She is Dom’s wife and a presence entangled in her dreams and regrets.

Mal is both a muse and a burden to Dom, his shadow haunts Dom’s dreams and plays an essential role in the film’s plot.
